About UniRank.one

This website started from a personal need. While searching for PhD and research positions, I constantly found myself diving into university rankings. I noticed a recurring issue: most ranking websites were cluttered and hard to navigate. I needed something simpler, a place where I could find clear and plain information quickly.

So, I decided to build UniRank.one.

My aim was to create a user-friendly site that stripped away the complexity and presented the essential data in an easily digestible format from the three longest established and most influential global rankings namely; Quacquarelli Symonds (QS), Times Higher Education (THE) and Shanghai Ranking Consultancy (the Academic Ranking of World Universities; ARWU).I began with the QS 2024 rankings as a base for the top 300 universities. With QS as the base for 300 universities, the corresponding rankings from THE and AWRU were compiled. Compiling the ranks from these 3 sources were a tricky affair because the names of universities were not uniform across various rankings and some universities were missing from some rankings(these are marked as ''N/A' in the table)

I hope UniRank.one helps you as much as it has helped me in making informed decisions about universities.

Happy university hunting!
